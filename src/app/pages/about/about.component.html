About us

Welcome to Kids Timer, a work in progress app by Pablo Paez. Our mission is to assist kids who have difficulty understanding the concept of time.

Instead of using numbers, we represent time using intuitive pictures and animations. This approach simplifies time perception, making it easier for children to manage tasks and understand how long activities take.

We are committed to continuous improvement, and we greatly appreciate feedback from our users. Your input helps us refine and enhance the app to better serve our community.

We value your feedback and invite you to join us on our journey. You can contribute by participating in beta testing, providing feedback, and spreading the word about our app.

For more information and to stay updated on our progress, please feel free to contact us via email at thepablopaez@gmail.com.

Thank you for your interest in Kids Timer. Together, let's empower every child to conquer time with confidence and joy.
